- [[Lesson 3]]
	- Definite article al is same as English definite article "the"
	- When al is added tanwin will be dropped
		- for example baytun -> al-baytu
	- Arabic letters are divided into two groups of 14, Solar and Lunar letters
		- Solar letters (Sun letters)
			- if the letter is solar letter, when adding al we don't pronounce al fully itself, instead it will be [[assimilated]] into the next word
				- for example - Al Shamsu becomes Ash-Shamsu, still no [[tanwin]] at the end since we have ash-shamsu and al-shamsu both using definite article al
		- Lunar letters  (Moon letters)
			- letter will be clearly pronounced
				- for example Al Qolam will still be clearly pronounced as Al-Qolam
