alias:: tamarbuta, Ta Marbuta, ta marbuta, round ta

- Ta Marbuta also known round ta is an arabic letter ta
- the phrase literally means "tied ta" or "bound ta"
- the reason why it is named like this is because of the fact that it looks like the normal ta letter got curved and connected and made this drop looking shape
- simply its the shape that gives this name
- most of the time this letter is used to convert masculine words into feminine ones
	- for example: muallimun, muallimatun
